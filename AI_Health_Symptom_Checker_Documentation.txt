AI Health Symptom Checker & Wellness Tracker

1. What is an AI Agent?
An AI agent is any system that perceives its environment through inputs, processes that information, and takes actions to achieve a specific goal. It operates in a cycle of perceive → think → act.
Our health assistant follows this exact cycle — it takes student health data as input, reasons about it using AI, and produces condition assessments and wellness recommendations as output.

3. How Our Agent Operates
The agent follows a simple 4-step cycle every time a student submits the form:
STEP 1 — PERCEIVE
Student submits Google Form with symptoms 
and lifestyle data
        ↓
STEP 2 — PROCESS
ChatGPT analyzes the input using the 
master prompt and knowledge base rules
        ↓
STEP 3 — REASON
Agent assigns probability % to each 
possible condition and assesses risk level
        ↓
STEP 4 — ACT
Agent outputs:
- Top 3 conditions with probabilities
- Remedy suggestions
- Personalized wellness tips
- Risk flag
- Wellness score
- Health disclaimer

8. Limitations
The agent cannot physically examine a patient
It relies entirely on self-reported symptoms which may be inaccurate
ChatGPT may occasionally hallucinate conditions not supported by symptoms
It cannot learn or improve from past predictions (no training loop)
Not suitable for diagnosing serious or chronic medical conditions
